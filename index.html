Who we are

550 Aero is an aviation safety technology company at the intersection of Silicon Valley AI and flight-line experience. We turn overwhelming aircraft data into earlier detection, clearer diagnosis, and recommendations designed for real cockpit workload.

Why it matters

Complex systems can be difficult to diagnose when the available data is dense, noisy, and time-critical. We’re building anomaly intelligence that synthesizes signals, context, and expertise into practical guidance—when it matters most.
